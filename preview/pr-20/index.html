About PEJSAM

We are IT professionals who believe that technical topics can be explored in creative and engaging ways. Through keynotes, workshops, and even theatrical productions, we combine our expertise in software development and quality to help teams collaborate more effectively.

Our work focuses on the intersection of development and testing, exploring how builders and breakers can work together to create better software and stronger teams.
